Design Principles: Kyle Southwick

White Space

Meta

Meta/Facebook does a great job at using the correct amount of whitespace. The padding is enough, but not too much to the point that everything is spread out.

Contrast

Forsgren

Forsgren website does a good job at making their website look good and readable through their contrast. It isn't anything flashy which is what I like. Simple, to the point, and does what it is supposed to do.

Repetition

Tesla

Tesla's website has a great use of repetition. As you scroll down the page it is consistent and you know what to expect as you go through. It goes product by product as you are scrolling and it is the same layout for each product. This makes it more user friendly and allows a better overall user experience.
