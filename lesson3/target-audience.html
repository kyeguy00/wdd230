Target Audience

The target audience for this website is anyone who is interested in Kanab and what it has to offer.

Personas

Persona 1: Tourist

I am a 53 year old that just retired and I am looking for my next big adventure. I came to this site because I have heard many great things about Kanab and heard that there is always something to do. Great scenary is my main reason for checking out this website.

Persona 2: Local

I am a 42 year old born and raised in Kanab. I came to this site because I love my town and I want to stay up to date with everything going on in Kanab!

Scenarios

"What will the weather be like near peek-a-boo trail?" "Who won the Kanab High School football game?" "Where is the nearest hike and how hard is it?" "Where is a nice cheap place to stay?" "Where is the nearest historic site?"
