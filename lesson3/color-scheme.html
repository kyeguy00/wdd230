Typography

Header Color

The header color will remain consistent throughout the website. It will be a linear gradient starting with white (255, 255, 255) at the top left and a dark red (71, 15, 15) to the bottom right.

Main

The main color for each page will either be white or grey. It may depend on certain things and is likely to change based off of the content. Overall, the main will be a lighter color and likely will be a gradient.

Navigation

The navigation colors will remain the same throughout the webpage except for when it is in a smaller view. The color will be white, hover background color will be a greyish color (122, 122, 122, 0.733) with a border of a reddish color (117, 0, 0). The smaller view will be a white color for the text and then black for the hover color.
